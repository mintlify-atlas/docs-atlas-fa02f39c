This guide walks you through the core workflow in SIC-HVD: logging in, navigating to capital investment requests, creating a draft, and submitting it for approval. By the end you will have a request in the `pending` state and ready for an approver to review.

<Note>
  You need an account with the **originator** role to create and submit requests. If you need to review and approve requests instead, you need the **approver** role. Contact your system administrator to get the correct role assigned. See [Roles and permissions](/user-guide/roles-and-permissions) for details.
</Note>

## Submit your first capital investment request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Open SIC-HVD in your browser. The application is served at the `/sic_hvd` path of your installation (for example, `https://yourdomain.com/sic_hvd`).

    Enter your email address and password on the login page, then click **Sign in**. If you have forgotten your password, use the **Forgot your password?** link to receive a reset email.

    After a successful login, you land on the **Dashboard**, which shows a summary of your requests and their current statuses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to capital investment requests">
    In the left sidebar, click **Capital Investment Requests** (SIC). This opens the requests index, which lists all requests you have created. If this is your first time, the list will be empty.

    <Tip>
      The sidebar only shows the sections your role has access to. If you do not see a section, your account may not have the required permissions.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new draft request">
    Click **New request** in the top-right corner of the index page. The request form opens in draft mode, meaning the request is saved locally without entering the approval workflow until you explicitly submit it.

    A request starts with status `draft`. You can save and return to a draft as many times as you need before submitting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the required fields">
    Complete the request form with the following information:

    - **Nombre del proyecto** — A short, descriptive name for the investment (`project_name`).
    - **Company and department** — Select the organizational unit making the request. These are configured by your administrator. See [Company and department management](/user-guide/company-department-management).
    - **Justification fields** — Describe the investment, explain why it is needed, document alternatives considered, and state the risk of not investing.
    - **Amount and exchange rate** — Enter the total requested amount in lempiras and the current exchange rate.
    - **Investment breakdown** — Select the investment type (purchase or construction) and fill in the corresponding cost fields. Select a budget option (A, B, C, or D) that describes the budget relationship for this request.
    - **Attachments** — Upload any supporting documents such as quotes, technical specs, or approval letters. See [Attachments and options](/user-guide/attachments-and-options).

    Click **Save** at any point to preserve your progress without submitting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send for approval">
    Once the form is complete and all required fields are filled, click **Enviar a aprobación** (Send for approval). You will be prompted to select the first approver — a user with the **Director Ejecutivo** function. The request status changes from `draft` to `pending`, and the selected approver receives an email and an in-app notification.

    You can monitor the status of your request on the [Dashboard](/user-guide/dashboard-and-reports) or in the requests list. The status column shows the current state at a glance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

After submission, the request enters the approval workflow. An approver will review your request and take one of the following actions:

- **Approve** — The request is finalized. A signed PDF is generated and you receive a notification.
- **Return** — The approver sends the request back to you with comments. You can revise it and resubmit, which moves it to the `resubmitted` state.
- **Reject** — The request is declined. You receive a notification with the reason.

For a complete explanation of each state and the transitions between them, see [Approval workflow](/user-guide/approval-workflow).

<Tip>
  Keep an eye on your in-app notifications (the bell icon in the top-right corner) and your email inbox. SIC-HVD sends notifications at each step of the workflow so you always know the current state of your requests.
</Tip>
