SIC-HVD is HVD's internal system for submitting, reviewing, and approving capital investment requests (SIC) and memo requests. It provides a structured approval workflow, role-based access control, real-time notifications, and PDF generation for finalized requests — all through a centralized Filament admin panel.

## Get up and running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy SIC-HVD">
    Follow the [installation guide](/getting-started/installation) or use the [Docker deployment](/getting-started/docker-deployment) to get the system running.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your organization">
    Set up your [companies, departments, and cost centers](/user-guide/company-department-management) to match your organizational structure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create users and assign roles">
    [Add users](/user-guide/user-management) and assign them as originators or approvers based on their responsibilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your first request">
    Log in as an originator and follow the [quickstart guide](/getting-started/quickstart) to create and submit your first capital investment request.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  SIC-HVD is an internal tool for HVD staff. Access requires a valid account with an assigned role. Contact your system administrator if you need access.
</Note>
